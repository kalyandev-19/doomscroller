ScrollStop
v1.0.0

Stop Doomscrolling. Reclaim Your Time.

ScrollStop intercepts Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts at a low system level using Android Accessibility Services and iOS Screen Time APIs. It leaves direct messages, standard posts, and long-form videos fully open while blocking only the addictive short-form video feeds.

Under The Hood Architecture

Android Accessibility Service

Scans the viewport node hierarchy to detect Reels/Shorts view IDs. Increments counters on vertical swipe gestures.

iOS Screen Time API

Utilizes FamilyControls and DeviceActivityMonitor to track duration/launches. Configures ManagedSettings shields.
